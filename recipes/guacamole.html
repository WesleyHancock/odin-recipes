Tasty Guacamole

Guacamole is a necessity when snacking with friends and family!
Guacamole is a creamy, avocado-based dip that's most commonly associated with Mexican food.
It's typically eaten with chips or as a topping on tacos or various other delicious meals.

Ingredients

  • 2 Avocados
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps of the process

  1. Cut the avocados into halves. Remove the seeds, and scoop out the pulp into a small bowl.
  2. Use a fork to mash the avocado.
  3. Stir in lemon juice, onion, salt, and olive oil. Cover the bowl, and refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.
