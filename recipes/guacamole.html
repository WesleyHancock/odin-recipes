Tasty Guacamole

Fresh Green Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 2 Avocados
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps of the process

  1. Cut the avocados into halves. Remove the seeds, and scoop out the pulp into a small bowl.
  2. Use a fork to mash the avocado.
  3. Stir in lemon juice, onion, salt, and olive oil. Cover the bowl, and refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.
